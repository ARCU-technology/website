About Us

ARCU.technology is dedicated to providing innovative curatorial solutions for digital collections drawing from years of proprietary research and expertise. We offer bespoke software solutions, tools, and in-house developed methods and models tailored to the domain-specific needs of a diverse client base, from large institutions to private individuals.

Our unique approach combines innovative techniques from multiple disciplines to foster creative and novel ways to navigate and curate digital collections. Seamlessly integrating visual and semantic analytics, we offer a fresh perspective on curating and interacting with digital collections, guaranteeing professional yet unconventional solutions.

ARCU.technology is a venture founded by Tillmann Ohm, a creative technologist, developer, researcher and artist, driven by innovation in the curatorial field. Since 2016, ARCU (Artificial Curator) and ARCU&OHM have developed numerous computational curation projects, carried out research on curatorial algorithms, and pushed the boundaries of digital collection innovation.

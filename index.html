Curatorial Innovations for Digital Collections

ARCU.technology blends years of curatorial expertise with interdisciplinary research, reshaping the exploration, curation, and mediation of digital collections through innovative technologies.

We Unlock Collections

… of cultural institutions, academia, industry and private collectors. Our projects are tailored to the needs of our clients and their unique collection data ranging from the development of novel digital tools and methods to the curation of entire exhibitions.

Our Services

AI Enrichment

We use state-of-the-art AI models to enrich your collection data with keywords, text descriptions, identified historic figures, transcription of handwriting and more.

Tools & Applications

We offer web applications, exploratory tools and interactive visualizations for your collections. Our tailored solutions are suitable for in-house research or public engagement.

Curatorial Experiments

We develop algorithms and computational methods for innovative curatorial projects to reveal semantic patterns and discover new connections in your collection data.
